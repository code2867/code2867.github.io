Hill Walking

My favourite hobby is walking/hiking. I have been doing it for years and go on a walk each week with DUHWS.

Here's a list of reasons why I love it:

  • It's a great form of exercise
  • You can meet new people
  • The great outdoors is a good break from everyday life
  • The views at the top of mountains are beautiful!
